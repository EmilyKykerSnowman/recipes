Regular recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup rice
3 green onions
4 eggs
2 heads of broccoli
2 hot dogs (optional)

Directions:

Set rice to cook about 20 minutes beforehand.
Start heating pan with olive oil.
Cut onions into small bits.
Put in eggs with onions.
Add salt & pepper.
Scramble the eggs.
Take out eggs, add more olive oil and let it heat for a bit.
Put in broccoli.
Add salt & pepper.
Stir fry broccoli til done.

Enjoy!
